e-Commerce workshop

Objectives

e-Commerce workshop is to facilitate audience to quickly understand China Ecommerce landscape, Unilever Ecommerce strategy and growth drivers and Ecommerce innovation and best practice to enable audience to apply learning back to their own brand to drive competitive and sustainable growth

How does it work?

China e-Commerce landscape,
Unilever e-Commerce vision,
Unilever Ecommerce strategy and growth drivers
Ecommerce innovations and trends
External Ecommerce best practice

For who it is?

Ecommerce workshop is Elite course for China BB, BD, Ecommerce and shopper marketing team. This course aim to uplift your Ecommerce digital skill

What are the outcomes?

  • Understands the importance of E-commerce not only as a sales channel but also for building brands
  • Understand market landscape and the basic elements that are required to win in E-commences
  • Inspired by Ecommerce innovation and best practice to drive seamless integration with all digital activities

Miscellaneous

Delivery Method Instructor-led course
Duration 1 day
Skills Improvement e-Commerce Knowing the landscape
Languages Chinese
Cost N/A
Owner E Commerce
