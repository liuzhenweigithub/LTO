DAP online

Objectives

Digital Acceleration Program Online (DAP Online) gives you the digital knowledge and skills they need to understand how key tools & channels work, and what marketers and their agencies do behind the scenes, to enable effective agency management.

How does it work?

Activities cover 8 topics:

1. Unilever Digital Roadmap
2. Web content
3. Search
4. Online media creative
5. Media planning
6. Social media
7. Mobile marketing
8. Strategy and media integration

For who it is?

DAP Online is used by both BD & BB to give the marketers he hands on knowledge necessary for daily job
For Marketing Foundation participants this is a mandatory part of their curriculum, the learners need to complete DAP Online to be allowed to join MF3 workshop.

What are the outcomes?

DAP Online creates strong underlying knowledge of key digital disciplines that translates into:

Better quality of digital execution
Better overall agency management
Better, informed integration of media channels
Better use of and alignment to existing UL approaches

Miscellaneous

Delivery Method Online guided self study
Online forum with moderation
Duration 14 weeks, runs at specific times during a year.
Skills Improvement Knowing the landscape
Strategic Thinking
Insights & measurement
Protection of digital assets and data
